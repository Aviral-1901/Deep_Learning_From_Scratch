# Attention
Every word can look upto everyother word directly so that the context is preserved and ambiguity resolution is done better.                                                                               At first we represent the words in vector form(embeddings), each word has its own different vectorial representation. Then we pass the words through different learned weight matrices of WQ, Wk and WV which gives us Q(query), K(key) and V(value).                                                               Then we use dot product to multiply Q and K to see how much the words are related and score for the matching is calculated. The quadratic nature for transformers come from this step because every word is matched again every other word so with every increase in word count the size for the memory increases quadratically. After scaling the scores (by dividing the scores with square root of dimension) to keep them stable. Then we convert the scores into percentages using softmax function. Now we know exactly how much attention each word should pay to each other. Finally the percentage scores is multiplied with V(value) matrix. This creates a weighted sum containing actual meaning of attended words together to get final output.
